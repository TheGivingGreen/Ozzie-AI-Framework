Year One · 2025
02 · The Legend of Year One

Soulard became
a golf course.

And somebody won a Green Jacket for their dance moves.

Last September, nine Soulard bars turned into nine holes. You showed up, grabbed a scorecard, and played the neighborhood. Sponsored drinks took strokes off. Skipped bars put them back on. By mid-round there were putting greens on bar patios and foursomes arguing par on Russell Avenue.

Every hole backed one of nine local charities. The sponsored drinks sold out at all nine venues. Ask the bar owners. They’re still talking about it.

$20,634 The course record. Set in 2025. Falls September 26.
