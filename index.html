Got Questions

FAQs

Everything you need to know before you tee off.

TGG is St. Louis's charity pub golf crawl. Nine bars in Historic Soulard become nine holes on a golf course for one Saturday. You carry a physical scorecard, play games at each hole, compete for the lowest score, and raise money for nine local charities at the same time. The most fun you will have on a Saturday and the most painlessly you have ever supported nine great causes.

Every ticket ($45) includes your official TGG hat which doubles as your wristband and souvenir, full access to all nine holes from 1-6 PM, your physical scorecard, $15 contributed to your chosen charity, access to mini-games at every hole, and Green Jacket eligibility. Your hat color determines which charity your ticket supports.

Hat pickup details will be sent via email in the week before the event. We will have a designated pickup location in Soulard. You must wear your hat to access venues on event day.

Each hole has a par and a featured sponsored drink. Order the featured drink and earn -2 strokes. Order any other drink and earn -1. Play the sponsored mini-game for -1. Skip a bar entirely and take +2. Carry your scorecard all day and turn it in at the end. Lowest total score wins the Green Jacket.

$15 from every ticket goes directly to your chosen charity. The hat color you pick at checkout determines which of the nine charities receives your contribution. In Year One we raised $20,634 across all nine partners. Year Two we are going bigger.

The event takes place across nine bars in Historic Soulard, St. Louis. All bars are walkable within the neighborhood. The full course map drops with the ticket launch. The official afterparty is at Paddy O's from 6 to 10 PM.
